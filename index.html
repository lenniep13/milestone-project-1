ABOUT

I'm a confident, creative designer. Currently working in a fast paced environment, meeting deadlines to a high standard. I'm hard working, adaptable, reliable, amiable and I get on well with everyone around me.

I have around eight years industry experience, in a variety of different studios around the world. Working in London, Hong Kong, Sydney and Pune, India. Most recently I spent 7 months training a brand new design team at the companies new office in India. This has allowed me to experience design trends within different cultures.

When I'm not creating awesome design, I enjoy listening to music, attending music events, working out at the gym and snowboarding. I love travelling, and experiencing other cultures. I often use my hobbies to inspire my design work.
